Privacy Policy

LeadHound respects your privacy. This page explains, in simple terms, how we handle information submitted through our website and lead generation enquiries.

Information We Collect

We may collect details you choose to send us, including your name, email address, phone number, business information, target market, and enquiry details.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide information about LeadHound services.
  • To assess lead generation requirements and prepare campaign recommendations.
  • To improve website, form, and campaign performance.
  • To manage business communication and customer support.

Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the website, manage forms, deliver campaigns, or communicate with clients.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information we handle, but no website or online service can guarantee complete security.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact sales@leadhound.net.
